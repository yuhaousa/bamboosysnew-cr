K12 Learning

Furen International School

At Furen International School, we combine the best of Chinese and Western education models, enriched by Singapore’s unique strengths. This distinctive approach makes us a top choice for international students who want a well-rounded, high-quality education in Singapore.

Challenge

As a preparatory school, Fu Jen International School (FIS) is committed to preparing students for university entrance and aims to become the most influential preparatory education institution in Asia. However, with the changing forms of education and students' learning needs, the traditional teacher-driven classroom learning model is no longer sufficient to meet the new challenges. Students need to engage in self-directed learning outside the classroom in order to explore subject content in depth, improve learning efficiency and develop independent learning skills.

FIS wanted to leverage technology to build an online learning platform that not only supports independent learning, but also ensures that students are on the right track through the provision of guided learning resources and online assessments to enhance learning outcomes. At the same time, the school needed a powerful management system to help teachers manage classes, track students' learning progress, and conduct comprehensive data analysis in order to provide students with personalized education support.

Solution

To meet FIS’s needs, a comprehensive online learning platform was designed and implemented, integrating tools for personalized instruction, targeted practice, and real-time monitoring to support students, teachers, and administrators.

User Management

The platform supports multi-role management for students, teachers, and administrators, enabling easy registration, secure login, and flexible permission control.

Course Creation and Learning

Teachers can create diverse courses tailored to instructional needs, while students can freely select and engage with courses. The platform supports multiple content formats, including text, video, and audio, to accommodate varied learning styles.

Knowledge-Based Practice

Teachers can assign practice exercises aligned with specific knowledge points, allowing students to engage in focused, targeted learning that reinforces mastery of key concepts.

Question Bank Creation and Management

Teachers can build and maintain structured question banks with clear categorization, facilitating efficient testing, assessments, and curriculum alignment.

Online Exams and Evaluation

The platform supports scheduled online exams with automatic grading and report generation, enabling students to assess mastery of key concepts while reducing administrative workload.

Progress Tracking and Data Analysis

Built-in analytics tools allow both teachers and students to monitor learning progress, performance, and other key metrics in real time. This enables timely adjustments to learning plans, improving both instructional effectiveness and learning outcomes.

Functional Scenario

Class Management

Teachers can create and manage multiple classes, grouping students for personalized instruction. The system automatically records attendance and learning activities.

Online Self-directed Learning

Students can browse and select courses of interest on the platform. The self-learning module offers abundant resources, allowing students to study at their own pace and improve learning efficiency.

Knowledge Point-based Learning

Students may focus on specific knowledge points according to their needs. The platform provides instant feedback and error analysis to help identify and improve weak areas.

Question Analysis Reports

Comprehensive statistics are provided, including the distribution of answer choices and correct/incorrect response rates. The system also summarizes common mistakes to highlight weaknesses in knowledge points.

Comprehensive Learning Analysis

Built-in analytics tools generate detailed reports on learning progress, test performance, and knowledge mastery, helping both teachers and students assess learning outcomes.

Exams and Assessment

The platform supports various assessments, including quizzes and final exams. It auto-grades submissions and generates performance reports to help students track their progress.

Result

The implementation of Furen International School’s online learning platform delivered measurable improvements across student learning, teaching efficiency, and institutional performance.

Increased Student Autonomy and Learning Efficiency

Personalized learning paths and self-directed resources empowered students to study independently and efficiently outside the classroom. This resulted in stronger learning habits, greater engagement, and improved mastery of key concepts.

Enhanced Teaching Effectiveness

Teachers were able to manage classes, courses, and assessments more effectively using the platform. Administrative tasks were reduced, allowing instructors to focus on individualized student support and tailored instruction.

Real-Time Performance Monitoring and Targeted Support

Progress tracking and data analytics provided real-time insights into student performance. Teachers could identify learning gaps promptly and deliver targeted interventions, resulting in more effective learning outcomes.

Strengthened Student Competitiveness

Structured online learning and assessments enabled students to monitor their progress, address weak areas, and enhance their readiness for future academic advancement, fostering highly motivated and competitive learners.

Flexible and Accessible Learning

Mobile learning applications allowed students to study anytime and anywhere, increasing learning flexibility and convenience. This contributed to continuous engagement and supported a more adaptable, modern learning environment.

Overall, the platform enabled Furen International School to successfully transition to a digital education model, aligning with modern educational trends and strengthening its leadership in preparatory education.
