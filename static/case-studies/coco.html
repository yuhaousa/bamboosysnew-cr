Coco Singapore Math Olympiad Learning Platform

Coco Education was registered in 2018 as a tuition centre for subject-based enrichment and Olympiad training. Despite the short duration of establishment, we stand on the track records of our teachers that have shown proven results in training hundreds of students over the past few years. Many of our students have demonstrated outstanding results in major competitions and have been selected for National Team Singapore for IMSO.

We are confident in helping students achieve their maximum potential. At the same time, we also hope that all the students will enjoy the learning experience with us as they increase their capability and interest in mathematics as well as other subjects.

Challenge

With the development of education digitalization, Coco Education has been gradually expanding its primary and secondary math training in Singapore, facing the need to improve the efficiency of teaching management. In order to cope with the complexity of course scheduling, individualized needs of students, and increasing pressure on operations management, Coco Education has partnered with Nine Bamboo Education Technology to develop a comprehensive online teaching and management platform. The platform aims to systematize Coco Education's operational processes, enhance students' learning experience, and further consolidate its competitiveness in the education market through an intelligent scheduling system and efficient paid operations management functions.

Solution

Operational and Administrative Optimization

In addition to enhancing learning outcomes, the platform streamlined academic and administrative operations through intelligent automation and data-driven management tools.

Intelligent Scheduling System: Flexible scheduling tools allow teachers to create and manage various class types aligned with their teaching plans. The system automatically arranges sessions, notifies students, and dynamically adjusts schedules based on student progress and teacher availability, ensuring optimal use of teaching resources.

Online Payment and Receipt Management: Integrated with multiple payment methods, the platform enables seamless online payments for parents and students. Automated generation of payment records and downloadable receipts simplifies financial management and improves transparency.

Course Booking and Management: Students can book courses online by selecting preferred time slots and locations for both online and offline classes. Real-time visibility into availability increases scheduling flexibility and improves the overall learner experience.

Operational Data Analytics: Comprehensive analytics dashboards provide real-time insights into enrollment, payments, scheduling, and resource utilization. These insights support data-driven decision-making and significantly enhance operational efficiency.

Functional Scenario

Intelligent Class Scheduling

Teachers can use the platform’s scheduling tool to set course time, location, and enrollment limits. The system automatically optimizes the timetable based on course requirements and students’ availability, and promptly notifies relevant students.

Online Payment

Parents and students can pay course fees through the platform, which supports multiple payment methods and automatically records payment details. The system generates electronic receipts, which users can view and print at any time.

Convenient course change managemen

Students can check their schedules and select suitable time slots within the week to reschedule classes. Once confirmed, the system automatically sends an email to administrators, reducing their workload significantly.

Operational Management

Administrators can monitor course enrollment, payment records, and student progress in real time. System-generated reports and charts support data-driven operational decisions.

Result

The implementation of the digital learning and management platform delivered measurable improvements across operational efficiency, student experience, and institutional decision-making at Coco Education.

Improved Operational Efficiency

Automated scheduling and online payment systems significantly reduced manual administrative work related to class arrangements and fee collection. As a result, management processes became more efficient and operational costs were lowered.

Enhanced Student Experience and Engagement

Students benefited from greater flexibility by independently selecting class times and completing payments online. This convenience led to higher engagement levels, improved satisfaction, and a more positive overall learning experience.

Stronger Data-Driven Decision-Making

Real-time operational analytics provided clear visibility into key performance indicators such as enrollment, scheduling efficiency, and payment activity. These insights enabled more accurate, data-informed decisions and continuous optimization of both teaching and operations.

Collectively, these results supported Coco Education’s successful digital transformation within the Singapore market, strengthening its competitiveness and positioning the institution for sustainable growth.
