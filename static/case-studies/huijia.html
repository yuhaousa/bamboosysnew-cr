Beijing Huijia Private School

Established in 1993, Beijing Huijia Private School has been preparing its students for success both in China and abroad for more than 30 years. The first International Baccalaureate (IB) World School in China, Huijia is known for its forward-thinking approach to education, integrating best practices from both Chinese and Western educational philosophies

Challenge

Meeting the Demand for Personalized and Intelligent Learning

Beijing Huijia International School (BHIS) is a leading school dedicated to providing international education, emphasizing the development of students' independence and self-driven learning spirit. However, with the increase in the number of students and the complexity of the learning content, it is difficult for teachers to provide personalized guidance for each student's learning progress. Under such circumstances, students' autonomy and planning ability in the learning process are limited and they are unable to realize their full potential. In addition, with the rapid development of education technology around the world, there is a growing demand from parents and schools for modern, intelligent learning tools to support students' independent learning through technology.

Solution

Enabling Self-Driven Learning Through Intelligent Technology

To address these issues, Beijing Huijia International School has developed an intelligent learning platform designed to help students realize self- driven and independent learning planning. The platform is designed to provide students with a flexible learning environment through technological means to help them master how to manage their own learning progress and goals. Key solutions include: Self-directed Learning Pathways: Students can select courses based on their interests and needs, and adjust their plans in real time. Progress Monitoring and Feedback: The platform tracks learning progress and provides personalized feedback and recommendations to optimize study strategies. Diverse Resource Support: A wide range of materials—including videos, e-books, exercises, and interactive content—meets learners' needs at different stages. Customized Interface Design: The platform adapts interface style and feature layout to Huijia’s preferences and user habits, enhancing the overall experience.

Functional Scenario

Modernized Interface Design

The platform adopts a modernized interface design, which is simple and intuitive and improves the user experience. The easy-to-navigate layout and responsive design ensure that students, teachers and administrators can operate smoothly on all types of devices, focusing on learning content rather than interface operation.

Self-Direct Learning Path Selection

Students can independently select learning modules of interest. Based on their choices, the platform provides relevant resources and recommended learning paths. If difficulties arise in a particular area, the platform suggests path adjustments to help them adapt and stay on track.

Real-time Feedback and Adjustment

The platform provides real-time feedback based on students’ progress and performance. If a subject falls behind schedule, it automatically alerts the student and suggests adjustments to the study plan. Parents and teachers can also monitor learning status and offer additional support through the platform.

Comprehensive Learning Resources

The platform integrates a diverse resource library, giving students on-demand access to multimedia courses, practice exercises, and discussion forums to enrich the learning experience.

Result

The implementation of Bamboo System’s intelligent learning platform delivered meaningful learning outcomes, strengthening student autonomy, engagement, and collaboration between home and school.

Stronger Independent Learning Skills

By setting personal learning goals and following structured learning paths, students gradually developed independent thinking and self-directed learning habits. This empowered them to take ownership of their learning and better prepare for future academic challenges.

More Personalized and Motivating Learning Experiences

Students were able to plan their learning according to individual interests and needs, resulting in a more personalized learning experience. This increased learning motivation, sustained engagement, and improved overall learning effectiveness.

Stronger Home–School Collaboration

The platform enhanced transparency between parents and teachers by providing clear visibility into student progress. Timely feedback and coordinated support fostered a closer and more effective home–school partnership.

More Efficient Use of Learning Resources

Access to rich learning resources and flexible learning tools enabled students to acquire knowledge more efficiently. This optimized the learning process during independent study and contributed to improved learning outcomes.

Application Value

Enhancement of students' independent learning ability: By setting their own goals and planning their own learning paths, students gradually develop the ability to think independently and learn independently to adapt to future learning challenges. Personalized learning experience: The platform allows students to plan their learning according to their personal interests and needs, providing a personalized learning experience that helps to increase motivation and effectiveness of learning. Enhanced home-school interaction: Parents and teachers can learn about students' learning progress through the platform and provide timely feedback and support, forming a closer home-school partnership. Efficient use of learning resources: Rich learning resources and flexible learning tools help students acquire knowledge more efficiently and improve their learning outcomes during independent learning
