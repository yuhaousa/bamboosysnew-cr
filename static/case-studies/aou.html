Sarawak Centre for Technical Excellence Talent Education Platform

The State Government, through Yayasan Sarawak, has established the Centre of Technical Excellence Sarawak (CENTEXS) in October 2014 to undertake the call by the Governor of Sarawak, His Excellency Tun Pehin Sri Abdul Taib Mahmud and The Right Honourable Datuk Patinggi Tan Sri (Dr) Haji Adenan bin Haji Satem, Chief Minister of Sarawak to train more technical people that the Sarawak state needs to industrialize the economy. Therefore, CENTEXS was assigned the tasks to train and upskill the young generation to meet the technical needs and requirements of the State industries focus on the trade of oil and gas, construction, manufacturing and mechatronics, and guarantee a career upon completion of their training.

Challenge

The Centre of Technical Excellence, Sarawak (CENTEXS), established in 1973 in Malaysia, focuses on providing vocational and technical training for school leavers and working students. With the rapid advancement of internet technologies and emerging industries in the 21st century, students face increasingly intense competition in the job market, while enterprises demand highly skilled professionals with recognized certifications. However, CENTEXS faces significant challenges: high costs of offline teaching limit accessibility and scalability; students lack access to high-quality online learning resources to enhance their professional skills; obtaining government- and industry-recognized certifications remains difficult; and there are limited channels for students to understand industry employment trends. At the same time, enterprises experience long recruitment cycles, struggle to quickly identify suitable talent, and lack transparent access to students’ academic performance and skill data. These challenges highlight the urgent need for CENTEXS to modernize its training and talent development model.

Solution

To meet the evolving needs of CENTEXS, Bamboo System, in collaboration with Huawei, developed the "JiuZhu Education Cloud Industry Talent Education Platform." This comprehensive online platform integrates vocational training, certification, enterprise recruitment, and talent management into a unified digital ecosystem. It provides diverse vocational and professional courses, supported by online exams and assessments to ensure mastery of key competencies. The system supports multi-role user management—including students, teachers, enterprise managers, and government agencies—enabling streamlined administration and collaboration.

The platform also enhances enterprise engagement by allowing companies to publish job vacancies, review candidate profiles, conduct video interviews, and access relevant skill data. Universities and training institutions can efficiently manage courses, student enrollment, academic activities, assignments, and certification issuance. Intelligent talent matching connects students’ skills with enterprise requirements, accelerating employment opportunities. Additional features such as live interactive classes, mobile application access, real-time messaging, forums, blogs, industry news updates, and advanced tracking and data analytics ensure continuous engagement, transparent performance monitoring, and data-driven decision-making.

Functional Scenario

Student Learning and Skills Enhancement

Students can register for vocational skills courses through the platform, study the relevant content online, and obtain government and industry-recognized certification upon passing exams, thereby enhancing their competitiveness.

Enterprise Recruitment and Talent Matching

Enterprises can post job openings on the platform and conduct rapid interviews with candidates through the video interview feature. The platform’s intelligent talent matching function can recommend suitable candidates based on enterprise requirements, shortening the recruitment cycle.

Resource Sharing Between Schools and Enterprises

Universities and enterprises can share courses and job information on the platform, promoting a seamless connection between education and employment.

Government Oversight and Policy Making

Government agencies can access detailed enrollment and employment data through the platform to understand industry trends and formulate effective policies to improve employment rates.

Result

Measurable Impact Across Education, Industry, and Government

Following the implementation of the JiuZhu Education Cloud Industry Talent Education Platform, CENTEXS achieved significant operational and institutional improvements. The shift to online education substantially reduced the cost of traditional offline training while expanding access to a wider student population across Sarawak. Digital delivery enabled scalable, high-quality vocational education without geographical limitations.

Students benefited from industry-aligned courses and recognized government and enterprise certifications, leading to improved employability and higher employment rates. At the same time, enterprises were able to streamline recruitment processes by directly accessing verified student performance data and skill profiles, significantly shortening hiring cycles and reducing recruitment costs.

On a broader level, the platform established an integrated education–industry–government ecosystem, aligning training with real market demands. This ecosystem strengthened workforce readiness, supported Sarawak’s industrial development, and enhanced overall employment outcomes. Through real-time data analytics and industry insights, government agencies gained better visibility into talent supply and demand, enabling more informed policy planning and strategic workforce development initiatives.

Application Value

Driving Sustainable Workforce and Economic Growth

Cost Reduction: By transitioning from traditional offline training to a comprehensive online education platform, CENTEXS significantly reduced operational and infrastructure costs while expanding access to vocational education across Sarawak.

Increased Employment Rate: Through industry-aligned programs and government- and enterprise-recognized certifications, students gained stronger professional credentials, improving employability and accelerating job placement outcomes.

Streamlined Recruitment Process: Enterprises benefited from direct access to student performance data and skill profiles, enabling faster talent identification, reduced hiring cycles, and lower recruitment costs.

Building an Educational Talent Ecosystem: By integrating schools, enterprises, and government agencies into a unified digital platform, CENTEXS established a skills- and employment-oriented ecosystem that aligns education with real industry demand.

Driving Industry Development: The platform strengthened workforce readiness and supported Sarawak’s economic growth by ensuring a continuous pipeline of technically skilled and certified professionals.

Improved Government Decision-Making: With real-time analytics and labor market insights, government agencies gained actionable data to design more precise employment strategies and long-term workforce development policies.
